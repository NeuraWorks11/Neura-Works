The Neura Works Workflow: Zero Effort, Maximum Efficiency

See how a single booking automatically handles all communications and management for you.

Step 1: Patient Books Online (The Start)

The patient finds your clinic online and uses your dedicated Neura Works booking link (integrated on your website or social media). They select a time, choose a doctor, and submit their details in less than 60 seconds.

**Benefit:** Ultra-low friction booking experience leads to higher conversion of leads to appointments.

Step 2: Immediate Verification & Confirmation (The Magic)

The moment the booking hits the system, Neura Works handles two critical tasks automatically:

Spam & Validity Check:

The system runs the patient details against our database to flag invalid numbers, bots, or known spam patterns. Only verified appointments proceed.

Instant Confirmation:

A personalized, branded confirmation message (WhatsApp/Email) is sent immediately, assuring the patient and formally securing the slot.

Step 3: Staff Visibility and Live Updates (The Outcome)

The new, verified appointment instantly populates the **Neura Works Dashboard**. Your reception staff sees the update in real-time. The system then automatically queues follow-ups, reminders (1 day before), and post-visit review requests.

**Staff Focus:** Your team moves from repetitive tasks (calling, typing) to high-value tasks (patient reception, billing).
